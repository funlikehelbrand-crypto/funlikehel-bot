## LONG BUSINESS DESCRIPTION (500 words / EN)

FUN like HEL was born on the Hel Peninsula — one of Poland's most famous wind destinations — and grew into something bigger. Today we operate two bases: the original school in Jastarnia (Poland, summer season July–August) and our winter base in Hurghada, Egypt (October–March), where the wind blows consistently at 15–25 knots and the water is warm, flat, and forgiving.

**Who we are**

We're a team of passionate kitesurfers who decided to turn their obsession into a profession. Our instructors are IKO-certified and have taught thousands of students — from nervous beginners standing on the sand for the first time to intermediate riders hunting their first clean jump. We speak Polish and English fluently, and our teaching style is direct, patient, and results-focused.

**Where we teach**

Hurghada is one of the world's best kitesurfing spots for beginners and intermediate riders. The lagoon conditions offer shallow, flat water, thermal side-shore winds, and consistent sessions almost every day. There are no currents, no rocks, and plenty of space to learn. It's the kind of place where progress happens fast.

In Poland, Jastarnia on the Hel Peninsula is a legendary kite spot. A narrow strip of land between the Baltic Sea and the Bay of Puck creates unique conditions: flat water on one side, waves on the other. Summer sessions here are pure energy.

**What we offer**

We teach kitesurfing from zero. Our beginner course follows the IKO methodology — the global standard for kite instruction — and covers kite control, body drag, water start, and first board rides over 3 days (9 hours on the water). By the end, most students are riding independently.

For riders who already know the basics, we offer progression courses focused on transitions, direction changes, and first jumps. Private lessons are available for those who want full attention and faster results.

We also offer Cabrinha gear test sessions — if you're thinking about buying a kite, come try it before you commit.

Our kite camps combine a week of intensive training with the social energy of a group retreat. Our stay + kite packages bundle accommodation with a full course — the easiest way to plan a kite holiday.

**The FUN like HEL difference**

Small groups. Maximum 4 students per instructor during group sessions. We don't run factory-style volume schools. We care about every student's progress and remember your name by day two.

Consistent wind. Hurghada's thermal wind cycle means sessions almost every day, October through March. No waiting. No wasted days.

Real gear. We use and sell Cabrinha kites — one of the world's leading brands. You'll learn on professional equipment, not outdated school gear.

Personal approach. We speak your language — literally (Polish and English) and in terms of teaching style. We explain things clearly and adjust pace to the individual.

## FAQ — HURGHADA BASE

**Q: When is the best time to go?**
October to March. Consistent thermal winds of 15–25 knots, water temperature 22–25°C, air temperature 25–30°C. You can kite almost every day.

**Q: Do I need a visa for Egypt?**
Most EU citizens including Polish passport holders can enter Egypt visa-free for up to 30 days or get a visa on arrival for a small fee. Check your country's requirements before traveling.

**Q: What is the cancellation policy?**
Free cancellation up to 7 days before the session. 50% charge within 7 days. No refund within 24 hours. In case of insufficient wind we reschedule at no extra cost — wind cancellations do not count as customer cancellations.

**Q: What if there's no wind?**
Hurghada has some of the most reliable wind on the planet — cancellations are very rare. If a session can't run due to conditions, we reschedule at no extra cost.

**Q: I've never kitesurfed before — can I really learn?**
Yes. Most students with zero experience complete our beginner course in 3 days and are riding independently by day 3. The Hurghada lagoon conditions (flat water, consistent side-shore wind, no current) are ideal for learning quickly.

**Q: Do you teach in English?**
Yes. Our instructors speak Polish and English. Other languages on request.

**Q: Can children join?**
Minimum age is 16. Under 16 is possible with written parental consent and depending on physical readiness.

**Q: What kites do you use?**
We teach on Cabrinha kites — one of the world's leading brands. We also carry demo stock for gear test sessions.

**Q: Can I book a lesson as part of my Hurghada holiday without a full package?**
Absolutely. Single lessons and short courses can be booked independently. Just pick your service and dates.
